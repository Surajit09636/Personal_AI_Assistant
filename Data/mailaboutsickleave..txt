Here is a sample email about taking a sick leave:

Subject: Notification of Sick Leave

Dear [Supervisor's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be taking a sick leave due to [briefly mention the reason for your absence, e.g. illness, doctor's appointment, etc.]. I am currently not feeling well and need to take some time to recover.

I will be unavailable from [start date] to [end date] and will do my best to catch up on any missed work as soon as I am feeling better. If there are any urgent matters that need my attention in the meantime, please let me know and I will do my best to address them.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding in this matter. If you need any further information or documentation from me, please don't hesitate to ask.

Thank you for your support and I look forward to returning to work as soon as possible.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
